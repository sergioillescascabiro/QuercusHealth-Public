# data/

Sample satellite imagery of the study zone (Spanish Dehesa, Extremadura).

| File | Date | Purpose |
|------|------|---------|
| `sample_2019aug.png` | August 2019 | Summer reference — full canopy season, La Seca symptoms visible |
| `sample_2024feb.png` | February 2024 | Temporal confirmation — trees dead since 2019 are now absent |
| `sample_dehesa.png` | — | Overview of the Dehesa landscape |

Images are captured from Google Earth Pro at zoom level 19 (~0.20 m/px over Spain).
Used in `notebooks/Phase2_Evaluation.ipynb` for multi-temporal ground truth validation.
